superposition

latest album under the minotaur project. released 2024-08-10. inspired by ryoji ikeda, ryuichi sakamoto, and others i've been listening to for the past 3 years, i wanted to create a project that had elements of those artists with my blend of rhythm and sound design.

this album is 5 tracks long with 2 remixes by Brayden Potts and myself.

majigen revisited: hsien-ko

spent 3 months learning how to combine musical ideas from the darkstalkers 3 OST with the music direction of street fighter III: 3rd strike to create this project. released on 2023-04-19, this album is a reimagining of hsien-ko's theme music through the lens of a soundtrack that has aged wonderfully.

special thanks to Evan Dawkins for the chords on HK.001

apple music | soundcloud | spotify

brayden potts & minotaur - versus mode

long-time friend and collaborator Brayden Potts joins me on a half-versus, half-collaborative soulhop tape. we began work on this roughly in march 2022 and released august of the same year. the album is split perfectly down the middle in terms of solo tracks and collaborative efforts. this project is one of my favorite works and was a great experience with a valued friend and peer.
